What are we all about?

Two artists, constantly studying sound, with influences ranging from every corner of the musical spectrum. From Metallica and The Strokes, to Gesaffelstein and Ott, their inspiration comes from a lot of very different genres.


Bouncy tracks are what this duo strives for, and lives off of. Some tracks harder and darker than others, all of them are quite serious in the low frequency region. Their tracks have grooves flowing from deep house, electro, and even some techno vibes mixed in.


Regarding live performance, Alex Lopex (Alphex) played drums in an indie band in high school called All Names Taken playing shows around the Sacramento, California area.


Bobby Green (B-Rip) played shows in the East Bay Area through Sacramento as a rhythm guitarist in a punk/ska band known as I.V.


Their transfer to electronic music, however, is something they’ve been working on for some time, and now they’re releasing it for the public’s free enjoyment.
